Speculators increase short dollar bets in latest week -CFTC, Reuters data
May 21 (Reuters) - Speculators increased their net short dollar positions in the latest week, according to calculations by Reuters and U.S. Commodity Futures Trading Commission data released on Friday.
The value of the net short dollar position rose to $15.07 billion in the week ended May 18, compared with a net short of $14.93 billion the previous week. (Reporting by Karen Brettell; Editing by Leslie Adler)
